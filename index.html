Metal Testing

Metal Testing Services

Comprehensive testing services for all metals — from basic mechanical properties to advanced corrosion and chemical analysis.

⚙️ Mechanical Testing

Determination of physical properties of metals using state-of-the-art servo-controlled testing machines.

  • Tensile Test (10g – 100 Tons, auto-graphing servo control)
  • Flexural, Compression & Shear Test
  • Charpy Impact Test (ASTM, NIST-USA calibrated)
  • IZOD Impact Test
  • Hardness: Rockwell (HRB/HRC), Brinell, Vickers (1–50 Kg), Micro-Vickers, Portable (D-Leeb)
  • Bend & Re-Bend Test
  • Tube Tests: Flattening, Flaring, Tensile
  • Fastener Tests: Bolts, Nuts, Spring Washers

🧪 Chemical Testing

A wide range of chemical testing and analytical services using advanced instrumentation:

  • Optical Emission Spectrometer (OES)
  • Positive Metal Identification (PMI) via X-Ray Fluorescence (XRF)
  • Atomic Absorption Spectrometer (AAS)
  • Carbon Equipment Analysis
  • Full Wet Chemical Laboratory
🌊 Corrosion Testing

🌊 Corrosion Testing

Macroscopic and microscopic corrosion analysis using industry-standard techniques:

  • Salt Spray Test
  • Inter Granular Corrosion Test – Practice A, B, C, E, F
  • Custom Corrosion Tests per customer requirements

🔍 Non-Destructive Testing (NDT)

First private NABL-accredited NDT lab in the combined states of Telangana & Andhra Pradesh.

  • Radiography Testing (RT) – Gamma Ray & X-Ray
  • Ultrasonic Testing (UT) & Thickness Measurement
  • Magnetic Particle Inspection (MPI) – Fluorescent & Non-Fluorescent (Prod & Yoke)
  • Liquid/Dye Penetrant Inspection (LPT/DP) – Fluorescent & Non-Fluorescent

🔭 Metallography

Well-equipped metallurgical laboratory offering a wide range of microstructure analysis services:

  • Macro & Micro Analysis
  • Grain Size and Flow Analysis
  • Inclusion Content Rating
  • Nodularity & Phase Analysis
  • Decarburization & Case Depth Measurement
Calibration

Calibration Services

Traceable to national and international standards. Ensuring your instruments provide accurate and reliable measurements.

📐

Dimensional Calibration

Tool & gauge calibration with traceable measurement chain. Instruments include:

  • Vernier Callipers, Micrometers, Snap & Plug Gauges
  • Height Gauges, Dial Indicators, Bore Gauges
  • Thread Measuring Instruments, Ring Gauges
  • Sine Bars, Clinometers, Protractors, Feeler Gauges
  • Extensometer, Microscope, Profile Projector, Test Sieves
  • Measuring Tapes, Scales, V-Blocks, Straight Edge & more
⚖️

Weight & Weighing Balance Calibration

Calibration of weights and weighing balances using E2 Class and F1 Class reference weights:

  • Weights of F1 Class and above
  • Balances with resolution of 0.01 mg (E2 Class)
  • Weighing balances up to 100 kg
🧫

Volume Calibration

Calibration of volumetric measuring instruments using 0.1 mg LC weighing balance:

  • Micro Pipette, Pipette, Burette
  • Measuring Cylinders, Conical Flasks
  • Beakers, Graduated Mugs, Volumetric Flasks
🔧

Pressure Calibration

Calibration of pressure measuring instruments:

  • Pressure Gauges (Analog / Digital)
  • Pressure Calibrators, Magnetic Gauges
  • Pressure Switches, Transmitters & Valves
  • Vacuum Gauges, Transmitters & Switches
🌡️

Thermal Calibration

Temperature & humidity calibration from -80°C to 1200°C:

  • Thermometers, Thermo-Hygrometers
  • RTDs, Thermocouples, PT100 sensors
  • Temperature & Humidity Data Loggers
  • Temperature Transmitters, Indicators & Switches
⚡

Electro-Technical Calibration

Electrical signal calibration with traceable reference standards:

  • Multi-Function Calibrators, Signal Generators
  • Temperature Indicators & Controllers
  • Strip Chart Recorders, RPM Indicators
  • Simulators and more

About Us

Our Story & Our Team

Established in 1999 as Hyderabad Engineering Labs by Managing Director Mr. Shivalingappa Kaginelli, who holds over years of experience as an approved BARC/AERB Radiological Safety Officer.

With a highly qualified, certified and professional team, we deliver best-in-class testing of materials and calibration of instruments using the latest state-of-the-art equipment, adhering to current National and International Standards.
